At RaftX Adventures, our mission is to boldly splash where no paddler has splashed before. We believe in the power of river-fueled adrenaline, unmatched teamwork, and slightly soggy socks. Whether you're chasing rapids or just escaping the office, our purpose is to guide every thrill-seeker down streams of glory and foam. Ride the waves. Embrace the wild. Live the motto: “Paddle Hard. Laugh Louder.”

History

Founded in the summer of 1998 with just two rafts and a fearless crew of adventurers, RaftX Adventures began as a dream to explore the untamed rivers of the northwest. Over the years, our passion for whitewater rafting grew into a full-fledged operation, guiding thousands of guests through scenic canyons and roaring rapids. From humble beginnings on the Snake River to epic journeys across the country, our legacy continues to flow strong — one wave at a time.
